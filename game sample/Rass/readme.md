## Game Design
>**Rass** tells a story about a hunter unexpectedly falls into a deep well during an exploration in the forest, leading to an unforeseen adventure. Deep beneath the ground, the hunter discovers ancient ruins that have been concealed for centuries, filled with lost technology. He find some powerful properties, such as a shotgun with strong recoil, a laser gun which can brake the blocks, and a flipper which can change the direction of gravity and set the whole world upside down. He will use this properties to fight with evil enemies and flee the plight.
